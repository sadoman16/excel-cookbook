                                                                                                                                     Track the flow of contacts and measure the effectiveness of lead generation tactics with Excel's Track Lead Conversion Percentages. This guide will walk you through setting up your data and calculating this critical metric for insightful analysis.

## Quick Reference
| Feature               | Description                                                                 |
|:----------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Syntax**            | `='Track_Lead_Conversion_Percentages'()`                                   |
| **Parameter**         | `Data`: Your Excel table or range containing lead information (e.g., status, source, date).                                |
| **Common Use Case**   | Calculating lead-to-opportunity or lead-to-customer conversion rates.       |
| **Output**            | A single percentage representing the conversion rate.                       |
